Our Story

Built on the belief that food should be honest.

FreshLink started with a simple question: why does a tomato that costs ₹10 at the farm in Nashik cost ₹50 on your table in Gwalior?

The Problem We Solve

The Chain Was Broken

The average vegetable in India passes through 5–6 intermediaries between the farmer and your plate. Each adds a markup. None add freshness.

Farmers were selling tomatoes at ₹8/kg. Consumers were paying ₹48/kg. The difference wasn't going to logistics — it was going to a broken, outdated middleman chain. FreshLink eliminates it entirely.

Founder Story

From Frustration to Responsibility

Hi, I’m Om Singh Tomar, Founder & CEO of FreshLink.

FreshLink didn’t start with comfort, planning, or certainty. It started with frustration — seeing a system where neither the supplier was satisfied nor the customer could fully trust what they were receiving.

I chose not to ignore it. I stepped in to build something better. What began as a small initiative quickly turned into a test of patience, consistency, and mental strength. There were days when everything went wrong — operations broke down, plans failed, and results didn’t match the effort. I’ve worked through harsh weather, long days, and unpredictable situations just to make sure the work didn’t stop.

I’ve faced losses. I’ve failed more times than I expected. And I’ve had moments where quitting would have been the easier option.

But I didn’t stop.

Because FreshLink was never just an idea — it became a responsibility. A commitment to build something that people can depend on. Every setback forced me to build stronger systems. Every failure made the foundation tighter. And every challenge shaped FreshLink into what it is today — not perfect, but reliable, resilient, and growing.

FreshLink has now been launched in Gwalior, marking the first step towards building a strong and dependable supply network. And this is just the beginning — we are actively working to expand across other cities in Madhya Pradesh in the near future.

Today, FreshLink stands for one thing above all — trust. Not promises, but delivery. Not words, but consistency. And we’re here to build something that lasts.

Om Singh Tomar

Founder & CEO
